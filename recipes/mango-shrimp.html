Mango Shrimp Ceviche

Description

This mango shrimp ceviche is the perfect summer dish. With crunchy cucumber, juicy tomatoes and mango, jalapeno, and lime juice, it's light, full of flavor, and a great combination of sweet and heat.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound: Cooked Large Shrimp, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 unit: Cucumber cut into bit-sized pieces
  • 1 pint: Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1 unit: Jalapeno Pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 unit: Fresh Mango - pelled, pitted, and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 unit: Lime, juiced, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons: Chopped Fresh Cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon: Chili Lime Seasoning (such as Tajin), or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine shrimp, cucumber, tomatoes, jalapenoes, mango, lime juice, cilantro, and Tajin seasioning in a large bowl. Taste; add more Tajin if needed
  2. (Optional) For more flavor, cover and refrigerate for 1 hour
